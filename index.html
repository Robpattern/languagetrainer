🎯 Heritage Speaker Accent Tips

⚠️ Retroflex Consonants

Heritage speakers often substitute flat sounds. Practice: zh vs z, ch vs c, sh vs s, r vs y/l. Key words: 需求 (xūqiú), 设计 (shèjì)

⚠️ Tone 3 Sandhi

Two 3rd tones in a row: the first becomes 2nd. 产品 sounds like chánpǐn not chǎnpǐn. Very common in business vocab.

⚠️ Final -n vs -ng

Don't conflate -n and -ng. 功能 (gōng néng) has -ng then -ng. 品牌 (pǐnpái) ends in -n. Keep them distinct.

⚠️ Neutral Tone

Business speech has fewer neutral tones than casual speech. Fully pronounce characters in formal terms: 产品经理 — all four characters get full tones in professional context.
