芒職聽茂赂聫 Retroflex Consonants

Heritage speakers often substitute flat sounds. Practice: zh vs z, ch vs c, sh vs s, r vs y/l. Key words: 茅聹聙忙卤聜 (x脜芦qi脙潞), 猫庐戮猫庐隆 (sh脙篓j脙卢)

芒職聽茂赂聫 Tone 3 Sandhi

Two 3rd tones in a row: the first becomes 2nd. 盲潞搂氓聯聛 sounds like ch脙隆np脟聬n not ch脟聨np脟聬n. Very common in business vocab.

Don't conflate -n and -ng. 氓聤聼猫聝陆 (g脜聧ng n脙漏ng) has -ng then -ng. 氓聯聛莽聣聦 (p脟聬np脙隆i) ends in -n. Keep them distinct.

芒職聽茂赂聫 Neutral Tone

Business speech has fewer neutral tones than casual speech. Fully pronounce characters in formal terms: 盲潞搂氓聯聛莽禄聫莽聬聠 芒聙聰 all four characters get full tones in professional context.
